We are proud to announce the launch of PT Victorindo Kimiatama's new official website at **victorindokimiatama.com**. This update is part of our ongoing commitment to improving the quality of information and service for our partners, customers, and prospective business partners.

## What's New

The new website features:

- **A more complete company profile** — our journey since 1978 to becoming a leading coating manufacturer in Indonesia.
- **Product & service information** across our three core sectors: automotive, marine/offshore/protective, and architectural.
- **Manufacturing capacity details** and information on our production facility in Karawang, West Java.
- **Quality certifications and standards** we hold, including ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001.
- **An easier-to-use contact form** for technical consultations and general inquiries.

The site is available in two languages — English and Bahasa Indonesia — to serve our business partners both locally and internationally.

We invite you to explore our new website and discover more about the products and services we offer.
